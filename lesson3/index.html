Purpose

Manta Chamber of Commerce web page exists to enable businesses in the region and the public in general to be updated with the endeavors of the Chamber of Commerce, which are: To help businesses raise their profiles, expand their networks, develop their knowledge and understanding of policy and opportunities they face, and be aligned with regulatory alignments.

This page will also help businesses and the public in general to be informed about the events program which can be around 300 a year.
